Undergraduate Dissertation Project

St. Xaviers College (Autonomous), Kolkata

Spring 2018

As a part of my undergraduate coursework at SXC, Dissertation and Data Analysis : ST-36543P, I worked under supervision of Professor Debjit Sengupta (SXC), on Time Series analysis of Gold price. The aim was to develop a predictive model to forecast the average monthly gold price, using Autoregressive process model fitting and backtesting with R and MINITAB.

keywords/tools used:Time Series, Autoregressive models, Minitab, R

IFCAM Winter School on Uncertainity quantification & sensitivity analysis of complex models

Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, IISER - Kolkata

Winter 2018

This winter school was organised by the Indo-French Center for Applied Mathematics and it was focused on implementation of Sobol indices in sensitivity analysis and practicing Kriging approach of metamodelling in Geostatistical domain.
