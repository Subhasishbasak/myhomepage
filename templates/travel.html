Mumbai

December 2019

Had the opportunity to visit the city of dreams also known as the finance capital of India. Our "Mumbai Darshan" started from the legendary CST station, then the Gateway of India, BSE, Marine drive and not forgetting the kebabs form "Bademiya". On day 2 we visited IIT-B, bandra, juhu beach and drove around the city with a Ford Figo zoomcar.

Pune

December 2019

Thanks to IFCAM for this sponsored trip and overwhelming hospitality at IISER-Pune. On a single day, we visited Shaniwarwada, Goodluck cafe and had the "best in town" Chitale-Bandhu Bhakarwadi. I still remember the mid-night rides we had on our Santro MT Revvcar, on the Mumbai-Pune national expressway.

Goa

October 2019

Beach and Booze : thats what Goa is famous for and my weekend trip was well focused on both! I strongly recommend to try the local Goan cuisine, specially the xacuti anf cafreal recipies, simply too good. We visited some legendary beach side cafes/pub viz. Souza lobo at Calangute beach and Brittos at Baga beach.

Pondicherry

August 2019

Needs no introduction. Its the most popular weekend destination from Chennai and a paradise for booze lovers. A straight 120 kms drive through the East Coast Road (NH-49) from CMI, which is probably the best cruising experience you can get in India. You will pass many stunning landscapes and scenic routes and enjoy the view of the sea, as you cruise down the ECR.
