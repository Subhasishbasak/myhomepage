Hello, welcome !

My name is Subhasish. I study data science at Chennai Mathematical Institute, India and I love it.

I was born and raised in Kolkata, the capital city of the state of West Bengal, India. After completing my high school I joined St. Xavier's College (Autonomous), Kolkata for a 3 years graduate programe in Statistics.

I like to fill up my life with learning new things, working on innovative ideas, and experimenting with data. I am sincerely dedicated to my education and work, and always try to see out any project to as close to perfection as is reasonably possible.

I have a passion for our traditional Bengali (both east and west bengal) culture and cuisine, and I care deeply about preserving its beautiful literature. In my leisure time, I love to cook different cuisines or sometimes enjoy long drives with loved ones ;p .

In this webpage I present my interests and the things I am working on. Now that you know a little about me take a peek around to know more about my experiences and projects.
